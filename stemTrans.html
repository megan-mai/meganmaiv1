STEM Transfer Community at UCLA, 2020

I was tasked with redesigning the website and branding for the STEM transfer community at UCLA. I worked with the existing site, which was hosted on WIX, to improve the user experience and to modernize the org’s visual identity. There were two constraints I worked under: I was required to keep the website on WIX, and I had to adhere to UCLA’s branding principles.

I also created graphics for various social media accounts, and redesigned the org's logos.
